About Myself

Hello! My name is Nikita and I'm Frontend developer. I have been working in this field for several years. It all started very simply: my family has a network of veterinary clinics and one day we really needed a web-site to provide the necessary information to clients (test results, price-list and important news). In the first month of work, I had to raise knowledge with the work of databases, adaptive layout of pages, and a little later backend part.

I like what I do and I give myself completely to my work.

  • Responsive Design

    Responsive web design is the practice of creating a website suitable for working on any device with any screen size, no matter how large or small, mobile or desktop. Responsive web design is centered around the idea of ​​an intuitive and enjoyable experience for everyone. A desktop computer and a user's cell phone, they all benefit from responsive sites.

  • Adaptive Design

    I always advocate for a modern approach to website development, so when I create a new site I always suggest the client and his adaptive layout. But this does not mean that sites without adaptive design need to be redone from scratch. If your site completely suits you and perfectly performs its functions, then you can simply adapt it to all screen resolutions using adaptive layout.
